About Dating Connection International

We are a human-first matchmaking service connecting single adults around the world. Our process combines a concise questionnaire with hands-on review by our matching team, followed by a WhatsApp call to finalize details. Privacy, safety, and authenticity are at the heart of everything we do.

Our Mission

To create a simple and effective matchmaking experience that values honesty, privacy, and genuine connection. We focus on quality over quantity and provide a human-reviewed matchmaking service.

Why This System Works

  • No endless swiping — meaningful questions that matter.
  • Human verification to reduce fake accounts.
  • Personalized matches reviewed by our team.
  • Final WhatsApp confirmation for clarity and trust.

Our Matching Process

  1. Explore: Browse profiles and learn how we work.

# Dating Connection International — Static Site This repo contains a production-ready static site for Dating Connection International. Edit `assets/profiles.json` to add profiles. Replace placeholder FORM IDs in `assets/script.js` with your Google Form entry IDs. ## Quick setup 1. Replace `YOUR_FORM_ID` and the `entry.` mapping in `/assets/script.js` with your real Google Form IDs. 2. Upload profile images to `/assets/profiles/` and update `assets/profiles.json`. 3. Push the files to GitHub and enable Pages on the `main` branch. ## Notes - The questionnaire collects answers client-side and redirects user to a prefilled Google Form for final storage (photo upload). Update mapping carefully.
